How to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps to permanently delete your Phantom Vault account.

Step 1: Log in to the Phantom Vault app.
Step 2: Go to the Accounts page from the settings menu.
Step 3: Tap on Remove Account.
Step 4: Confirm your choice to Delete Account.
