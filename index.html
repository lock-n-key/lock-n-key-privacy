Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Kronos Capital ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the mobile application Lock & Key ("the App").

Lock & Key allows users to purchase subscriptions, upload and manage photos and videos, organize them into albums, and retrieve or download them at any time. For privacy purposes, users may customize the app's name and icon (e.g., to resemble a calculator or calendar).

1. Information We Collect

  • User Content: Photos, videos, and album information uploaded by the user.
  • Subscription Data: Information related to payment status and purchase history (handled by third-party processors like Google Play, Apple, or Stripe).
  • Device Data: Device model, operating system, and crash logs used to improve app stability and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core features including uploading, organizing, retrieving, and downloading user content.
  • To verify active subscriptions and unlock premium features.
  • To maintain app performance, security, and user experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

All user data is securely stored and protected using modern encryption techniques. We implement safeguards to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party providers (such as cloud storage, analytics tools, or payment processors) that adhere to standard data protection policies. These services are used only as necessary and in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

5. User Controls

  • Users can delete uploaded content directly from the app.
  • Subscriptions can be managed or canceled via the app store platform used for purchase.
  • Users may customize the app name and icon via in-app settings for additional privacy.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and significant changes will be communicated through the app if necessary.
